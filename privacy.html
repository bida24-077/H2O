Privacy Policy

Last Updated: [25 May 2025]

1. Introduction

Water Conservation Initiative ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our water conservation website.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, and contact details when you voluntarily provide them
  • Usage Data: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and access times
  • Cookies: Website usage data through cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and improve our water conservation services
  • Respond to inquiries and provide educational resources
  • Send newsletters and conservation updates (with consent)
  • Analyze website usage patterns

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell or trade your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers assisting with website operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Third parties with your explicit consent

5. Data Security

We implement security measures including encryption and secure servers to protect your information. However, no electronic transmission is completely secure.

6. Your Rights

  • Access and request copies of your data
  • Request correction or deletion of information
  • Opt-out of communications
  • Disable cookies through browser settings

7. Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Enhance user experience
  • Analyze traffic patterns
  • Remember preferences

8. Policy Changes

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

For privacy-related inquiries, contact us at:
